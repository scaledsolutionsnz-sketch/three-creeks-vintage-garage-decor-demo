Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Three Creeks Vintage Garage Decor ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Submit an enquiry or contact form on our website
  • Leave a review or feedback via our review system
  • Contact us by phone or email
  • Visit our premises

The information collected may include your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide information about our products and services
  • Process transactions and arrange purchases
  • Improve our products, services, and customer experience
  • Contact you regarding your enquiry or visit

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Our contact forms are powered by StaticForms, a third-party form processing service. Your form submission data is processed according to their privacy policy. We do not store this data on our own servers beyond what is necessary.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you, and to request correction of any inaccurate information. To exercise these rights, contact us directly.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
