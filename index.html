Our Story

Where Petrol Culture Meets Passionate Collecting

Tucked into the heart of Burkes Pass in the Mackenzie Country, Three Creeks Vintage Garage Decor is more than a shop -- it's a destination for anyone who loves the golden age of motoring and the art of the garage.

Dave Taylor has spent decades hunting down the finest petroliana, enamel signage, oil cans, vintage automotive artwork, and rare garage memorabilia from across New Zealand and around the world. Every piece in the collection tells a story.

Whether you're a serious collector hunting a specific piece, or you're simply looking for something to make your man-cave or classic car garage look the part -- Three Creeks is a place you won't want to leave in a hurry.

Burkes Pass, Mackenzie Country

A Destination in Every Sense

Three Creeks sits in one of the most scenic corridors in New Zealand -- the road through Burkes Pass that connects Christchurch to the Mackenzie Basin, Tekapo, and beyond. Every year, thousands of travellers pass through this gateway to the high country.

Dave Taylor has turned a lifelong passion for motoring history into one of New Zealand's most unique retail experiences. The property is as much a museum as a shop -- a living, breathing archive of petrol culture and garage heritage.

  • Decades of specialist collecting knowledge
  • Constantly refreshed stock from NZ and overseas
  • Located on the main tourist route through Burkes Pass
  • Perfect stopping point for car clubs and touring groups
  • Private viewings and events available on request
